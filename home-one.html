Home services

What type of service we provide?

  1. Emergency Repairs: Prompt response to urgent maintenance issues such as plumbing leaks, electrical problems, heating or cooling failures, or security concerns to ensure the safety and well-being of tenants.
  2. General Maintenance: Regular upkeep and maintenance tasks to keep the rental property in good condition, such as changing air filters, inspecting and repairing appliances, checking for leaks, and maintaining the landscaping.
  3. Appliance Repair: Repairing or replacing malfunctioning appliances such as refrigerators, stoves, dishwashers, washing machines, and dryers to ensure tenants have access to essential amenities.
  4. Plumbing Services: Fixing plumbing issues such as clogged drains, leaking faucets, broken pipes, or water heater malfunctions to ensure tenants have access to clean water and functioning plumbing fixtures.
  5. Electrical Services: Addressing electrical problems such as faulty wiring, power outages, broken light fixtures, or malfunctioning outlets to maintain a safe and functional living environment.
  6. HVAC Services: Repairing or servicing heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems to ensure tenants have proper climate control and indoor air quality.
  7. Locksmith Services: Replacing or repairing locks, keys, and security systems to ensure the security and privacy of tenants' homes.
  8. Carpentry and Handyman Services: Addressing minor repairs and maintenance tasks such as door and window repairs, drywall patching, painting, or installing shelving and fixtures.
  9. Pest Control Services: Addressing pest infestations such as ants, rodents, termites, or bedbugs to maintain a clean and hygienic living environment.
  10. Cleaning Services: Providing occasional or deep cleaning services for common areas or specific rooms within the rental property to ensure cleanliness and sanitation.
